REQUIREMENTS

For High School Graduates

  1. Report Card (Form 138)
  2. Certificate of Good Moral Character
  3. Two pcs. 1×1 and 2×2 pictures w/ red back ground

For Transferees

  1. Transcript of records or true copies of grades
  2. Honorable Dismissal
  3. Certificate of Good Moral Character
  4. Letter of Application of the Student
  5. Letter of Parental Consent
  6. Two pcs 1×1 and 2×2 pictures w/ red background

Foreign Students

  1. Photocopy of the Alien Certificate of Registration
  2. Previous Study Permit (If Transferee From Other Philippine Schools)
  3. Accomplished Personal History Statement
  4. Certificate of Bank Deposits
  5. Affidavit of Support
  6. Birth Certificate of Passport
  7. Transcript of Records
  8. High School Card
  9. Seven (7) Copies of 2×2 Pictures
  10. Two (2) Copies of 1×1 Pictures (Items 3-10 are needed for Student Visa Application)
  11. Continuing Foreign Students must meet the same Requirements as of Filipino Students in Addition to the above mentioned requirements
